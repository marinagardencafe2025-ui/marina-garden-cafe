About Us

Marina Garden Café is a garden-inspired restaurant located in MEPZ 1, F. Martir Street, Ibo, Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu.

The café provides a relaxing environment surrounded by greenery, offering both indoor and outdoor dining spaces. Guests can enjoy Filipino and international dishes, fresh seafood, grilled specialties, pasta, and refreshing drinks.

The restaurant is also a popular venue for birthdays, family gatherings, and casual dining experiences.

Average Dining Cost: ₱400 – ₱600 per person

Mission & Vision

To provide guests with high-quality food and excellent service in a relaxing garden-style environment, while promoting fresh ingredients, local flavors, and memorable dining experiences for families, friends, and visitors.
To become a leading dining destination known for its beautiful garden ambiance, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality, creating a place where people can enjoy nature, good food, and meaningful moments together.
